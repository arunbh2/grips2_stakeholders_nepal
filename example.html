Introduction

Overview of GRIPS2 (Grid Resilience through Intelligent Photovoltaics and Storage), a UNIDO-supported pilot project in Nepal for a >1 MW smart solar-storage industrial microgrid[cite: 1].

Aims to demonstrate sustainable energy replacing diesel and unreliable grid power for industry, emphasizing knowledge-sharing and community engagement as an A2D Facility "lighthouse" project[cite: 2, 3].

This report provides the research foundation for building the GRIPS2 innovation ecosystem[cite: 4].
